    INGREDIENTS
    4 slices bacon
    2 slices white bread
    2 leaves iceberg lettuce, fresh and full
    1 slice of ripened tomatoes
    2 tablespoons mayonnaise



    DIRECTIONS
    Cook bacon until crispy, then drain on paper towels.
    Toast the 2 slices of bread.
    Spread 1 tablespoon mayo on each slice of toasted bread. (More or less, to taste).
    Add 1 slice of lettuce to 1 pieces of mayo-spreaded toast.
    Add 1 slices of tomato on top of lettuce.
    Arrange 4 slices of bacon evenly on top of tomato. (Break bacon slices in half to fit, if needed)
    Put the remaining pieces of mayo-spreaded toast on top to finish the sandwiches.
    Enjoy!
